    Tetris is a tile-matching puzzle game, where the player must rotate and move falling pieces (tetrominoes) that fall faster and faster. The game ends when the pieces reach the top of the screen. The goal is to get as many points as possible by clearing lines of blocks. The more lines cleared at once, the more points you get.
